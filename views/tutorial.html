Radiology Test App Tutorial

Thank you for participating in this research project. You will be taking two types of exams over the course of the next hour that will help evaluate your capacity at perceiving lung nodules. The layout of the exam is as follows.

Non-timed:

You shall be presented with ## of images which will be listed on the left of the screen. For each image look for the abnrormality if it exists. Once you click on the abnormality, on the right-hand-side, the image will be labeled as marked. For every finding you click on, a popup box will appear and you will be asked to grade how confident you are the finding is abnormal. Once you click, a slider box will pop-up and you can slide the box to define your certainity from 0-100%. Once you are satisfied, press ok on that box. Some presented images are normal and do not have any abnormal findings. If you don't see any findings, mark the radiograph as Normal. You will not be able to progress until all the images have been marked.
Here goes a nontimed tutorial gif

Timed:

This portion of the exam will test your ability to quickly detect abnormalities on chest radiographs (your Gestalt). You shall be presented with 60 images that will sequentially load every 5 seconds. There is a timer in the upper right corner which will display the time left per image and how many images are left. There will be normal images also presented. If you don't see an abnormality, wait for the next image to load without clicking on the image. If you see an abnormality, remember, you only have 5 seconds to click and mark it!
